02 / ABOUT

Who
I am.

UTD · Class of 2026

I'm a double major in Computer Science and Biology at UT Dallas — two fields that, to me, aren't opposites but extensions of the same curiosity. I build software the same way I study life: by understanding systems from the inside out.

My love for Biology runs deep. Growing up watching my father — an MD specializing in gastroenterology — help patients every single day showed me early on what it means to dedicate your life to something bigger than yourself. That stayed with me.

Today I'm channeling that into dentistry, actively shadowing dentists to understand the craft firsthand. I'm drawn to how dentistry blends precision, science, and genuine human care — the same qualities I try to bring to everything I build.
